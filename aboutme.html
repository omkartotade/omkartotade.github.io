ABOUT ME

"You are the hero of your own story!"

I am from Mumbai, India and I currently live in the Phoenix, Arizona area in the United States. I work as a Software Engineer with a company in Tempe, creating web applications, services and mobile apps. I moved to Tempe in August 2015 to pursue my Master's degree at Arizona State University. After 2 years, 4 semesters, 10 classes, 3 dozen assignments and numerous sleepless nights, I graduated with a Master of Computer Science degree in May 2017. I did my Bachelor's in Computer Engineering from the University of Mumbai and graduated in May 2014.

I believe in maintaining my work and life balance pretty well, which is why I cool myself down by exercising and working out. I believe in the saying, "A Healthy Body has a Healthy Mind". Lifting weights destresses me and makes me energetic! I love watching Soccer and Cricket as well, and you can find me playing FIFA on my XBox a lot! I am a huge Cristiano Ronaldo fan, he was the reason I started following soccer, and naturally I like Real Madrid FC!

I am usually hesitant to start watching a new TV show, because I get engrossed in it so much, that I end up binge watching it. My favorite TV show is F.R.I.E.N.D.S. and I quote a lot from it in my everyday life. I mean, could this show BE any better? I also enjoy watching Game of Thrones a lot and I obsess over it! Some of my other favorite shows include, The Big Bang Theory, Suits, Riverdale and I also love watching Stand-up comedy gigs on Netflix! I also watch Bollywood movies, because they provide good entertainment and because I am from India and I enjoy that stuff!

I am here to create a stereotype of a geek who works out, enjoys sports and loves watching TV!
